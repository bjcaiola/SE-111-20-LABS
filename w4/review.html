Review of RocketMan

By: Bradley Caiola
April 19th, 2025

Disney's 1997 RocketMan is a low-budget, unserious comedy. I first saw the movie when I was a child and thought the randomness of the movie was humerous. Now, being older, the movie is even funnier. There are several small funny bits that are referenced throughout the movie and the cluelessness of Fred Randall in contrast to his crewmates adds a seriously funny dynamic between them. The more you watch, the funnier it gets. If you're looking for a good laugh without needing to think about it, this is a great movie for you.
