Bio

Bradley Caiola is a 17-year old Cybersecurity major at New England Institute of Technology. In addition to his love for computers, he also works as a Sailing Instructor at the Mystic Seaport Museum in Mystic, CT and does a plethora of technical theatrics at The Granite Theatre in Westerly, RI. He currently lives in Richmond, RI where he has lived for his whole life. He has five older siblings, three brothers and two sisters, and one (soon to be more) sister in law, a dog, and two cats. In his free time, Bradley enjoys working on computer projects, working, sailing, fishing, and educating himself on current technologies.
